Warranty Information

Quality you can count on

3

On materials and workmanship for all Peekaboo Shades products

Warranty Coverage

Peekaboo Shades provides a 3-year limited warranty from the date of purchase covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal residential use.

What's Covered

  • Fabric defects (seams, tears, discoloration from manufacturing)
  • Operating mechanisms (springs, clutches, chains)
  • Roller tubes and end caps
  • Motorized components (motors, remotes)
  • Mounting hardware
  • Valances and fascias
  • Cordless lift mechanisms

What's Not Covered

  • Normal wear and tear
  • Damage from improper installation
  • Damage from misuse or accidents
  • Fading from sun exposure
  • Pet or child damage
  • Commercial use applications
  • Products modified after purchase

Motor Warranty

Motorized shades include an extended warranty on the motor components:

  • AOK Motors: 3-year warranty
  • Dooya Motors: 3-year warranty
  • Smart Hub: 1-year warranty
  • Rechargeable Batteries: 1-year warranty (batteries are consumables and naturally degrade over time)

How to Make a Warranty Claim

If you believe your product has a defect covered under warranty:

  1. Contact us at info@peekabooshades.com or (469) 758-8935
  2. Provide your order number and date of purchase
  3. Describe the issue in detail
  4. Send photos or videos showing the defect
  5. We'll assess the claim and respond within 2-3 business days

Warranty Resolution

For valid warranty claims, we will, at our discretion:

  • Repair the defective component
  • Replace the defective component or entire product
  • Provide parts for self-repair (for simple fixes)

Replacement products are covered for the remainder of the original warranty period.

Care & Maintenance

To keep your shades in good condition and ensure warranty coverage:

  • Dust regularly with a soft cloth or vacuum with brush attachment
  • Operate shades gently - don't yank or force the mechanism
  • Keep motorized shades charged to prevent motor strain
  • Avoid excessive moisture or direct water exposure
  • Don't use harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners

Proof of Purchase

Warranty claims require proof of purchase. We recommend keeping your order confirmation email, which contains your order number and purchase date. We also maintain purchase records in our system.
