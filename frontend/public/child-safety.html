Your family's safety is our top priority. All Peekaboo Shades products are available in cordless or motorized options.

Window Cord Hazards

The U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC) has identified window covering cords as one of the top hidden hazards in American homes. Young children can become entangled in looped cords, leading to strangulation. Since 1990, hundreds of children have died from window covering cord accidents.

Our Commitment to Safety

At Peekaboo Shades, we believe no family should have to choose between beautiful window treatments and their children's safety. That's why every shade we sell is available in a cord-free option - either cordless (spring-lift) or motorized.

Child Safety Standards

Our products comply with current CPSC and ANSI/WCMA (Window Covering Manufacturers Association) safety standards:

  • ANSI/WCMA A100.1 - American National Standard for Safety of Corded Window Covering Products
  • Products tested and certified for cordless operation
  • No accessible cords that could form a loop around a child's neck

Making Your Home Safer

Home Safety Checklist

  • Replace all corded blinds with cordless or motorized alternatives
  • If you must keep corded blinds temporarily, use cord cleats to keep cords out of reach
  • Move cribs, beds, and furniture away from windows with cords
  • Cut looped cords into two separate cords
  • Keep continuous-loop cords tight and anchored to the wall
  • Don't assume children can't reach cords - they're resourceful climbers
