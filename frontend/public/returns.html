Returns & Refund Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority

Satisfaction Guarantee

We stand behind the quality of our products. If something isn't right, we'll make it right.

Custom Products Policy

Because all Peekaboo Shades products are custom-made to your exact specifications (dimensions, fabric, color, and options), we cannot accept returns for:

  • Change of mind or preference after ordering
  • Incorrect measurements provided by the customer
  • Color variations due to monitor/screen differences
  • Products that have been installed

Measure Carefully

Please double-check your measurements before ordering. We're happy to review your measurements before you place an order - just contact us. Once production begins, changes cannot be made.

When We Accept Returns

We accept returns and provide full refunds in the following situations:

  • Manufacturing defects - Issues with materials, construction, or finish
  • Incorrect product received - We sent the wrong size, color, or product type
  • Damaged during shipping - Product arrived damaged (report within 48 hours)
  • Operational issues - Shade doesn't function properly (motor doesn't work, spring mechanism fails, etc.)

Return Process

1

Contact Us

Email info@peekabooshades.com or call (469) 758-8935 within 14 days of receiving your order. Describe the issue and include photos if applicable.

2

Get Return Authorization

We'll review your request and, if approved, provide a Return Authorization (RA) number and return instructions.

3

Ship the Product

Package the product securely (original packaging preferred) and ship to our facility. For defects and our errors, we provide prepaid return labels.

4

Receive Refund or Replacement

Once we receive and inspect the return, we'll process your refund or ship a replacement within 5-7 business days.

Refund Timeline

Refunds are processed to your original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days after processing
  • PayPal: 3-5 business days after processing
  • Store Credit: Immediate (if preferred)

Exchanges

If you'd like to exchange a defective product for a different size or style, contact us. We'll work with you to find the best solution, which may include:

  • Replacement with the same product (for defects)
  • Store credit for a different product
  • Partial refund for a lesser-priced alternative

Damaged Shipments

If your package arrives damaged:

  1. Take photos of the damaged packaging and product immediately
  2. Do not discard the packaging materials
  3. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  4. We'll file a claim with the carrier and send a replacement
