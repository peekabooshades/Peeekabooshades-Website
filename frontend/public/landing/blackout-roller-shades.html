Sleep Better with Blackout Shades

Blackout roller shades are the ultimate solution for creating a dark, restful environment. Whether you're a shift worker who needs to sleep during the day, have a nursery that needs complete darkness for nap time, or want to enjoy movies without screen glare, blackout shades deliver.

Our premium blackout fabrics block 99%+ of incoming light when properly installed. The special backing prevents light from penetrating while the front fabric comes in your choice of colors and textures to match your decor. Unlike cheap blackout curtains that let light seep around the edges, our custom-fitted roller shades provide edge-to-edge coverage.

In hot Texas summers, blackout shades do double duty by blocking solar heat gain through your windows. This can significantly reduce your cooling costs while keeping your rooms comfortable. The insulating properties also help in winter by reducing heat loss through windows.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do blackout shades really block all light?
Our blackout shades block 99%+ of light. For complete darkness, we recommend inside mount installation with side channels to minimize light gaps around the edges.
Are blackout shades good for sleep?
Yes! Blackout shades are excellent for sleep as they create a dark environment that helps regulate your circadian rhythm. They're especially beneficial for shift workers, light-sensitive sleepers, and nurseries.
Do blackout shades keep rooms cooler?
Blackout shades provide excellent insulation, blocking both light and heat. They can help reduce cooling costs in hot Texas summers by preventing solar heat gain through windows.
What colors do blackout shades come in?
Our blackout fabrics come in a wide range of colors from bright white to deep charcoal. The blackout backing is hidden behind the decorative front fabric.
