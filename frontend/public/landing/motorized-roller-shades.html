Frequently Asked Questions

How do motorized shades work?
Motorized shades have a small motor in the roller tube that raises and lowers the shade. They can be controlled via remote, smartphone app, voice assistants, or scheduled automations.
Do motorized shades need to be plugged in?
We offer both battery-powered and hardwired options. Battery motors last 6-12 months on a charge and can be recharged via USB. Hardwired motors require electrical installation but never need charging.
Do your motorized shades work with Alexa?
Yes! With our Smart Hub accessory, you can control your motorized shades with Alexa, Google Home, Apple HomeKit, and other smart home systems.
What's the difference between AOK and Dooya motors?
Both are premium motor brands. AOK motors are known for their reliability and value, while Dooya motors offer slightly more torque for larger shades. Both work with our Smart Hub for voice control.
