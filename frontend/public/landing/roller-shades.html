Frequently Asked Questions

What are roller shades?
Roller shades are window coverings made from a single piece of fabric that rolls up onto a tube at the top of the window. They offer a clean, modern look and come in various light-filtering options from sheer to complete blackout.
How much do custom roller shades cost?
Custom roller shades from Peekaboo Shades start at around $45-65 for smaller windows and range up to $200-350 for larger windows with motorization. Pricing depends on size, fabric choice, and features like cordless or motorized operation.
Are roller shades good for bedrooms?
Yes! Blackout roller shades are excellent for bedrooms as they block 99%+ of light, helping you sleep better. They're also great for nurseries, home theaters, and any room where light control is important.
Do you deliver roller shades to Dallas and Austin?
Yes! Peekaboo Shades offers free shipping throughout Texas, including Dallas, Austin, Houston, San Antonio, and Fort Worth. Most orders arrive within 2-3 weeks of ordering.
Can roller shades be motorized?
Absolutely! We offer motorized roller shades with both battery-powered and hardwired options. Our motorized shades work with smart home systems and can be controlled via remote, app, or voice assistants.
