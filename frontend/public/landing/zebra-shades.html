The Beauty of Zebra Shades

Zebra shades, also known as dual shades or transitional shades, are a stunning modern window treatment that combines the functionality of blinds with the elegance of shades. Their distinctive alternating stripes of sheer and solid fabric create a contemporary look that complements any interior design.

What makes zebra shades unique is their innovative dual-layer construction. By simply adjusting the shade, you can align the solid stripes for complete privacy and light blockage, or offset them to allow filtered natural light while maintaining privacy. This versatility makes them perfect for Texas homes where managing intense sunlight is essential.

Our custom zebra shades are made to your exact window measurements and come in a variety of colors and textures. From soft neutrals to bold statements, we have the perfect zebra shade to match your decor. All our zebra shades feature premium fabrics and smooth-operating mechanisms for years of trouble-free use.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are zebra shades?
Zebra shades (also called dual or transitional shades) feature alternating bands of sheer and solid fabric. By adjusting the alignment of these bands, you can seamlessly transition from filtered light to privacy or complete light blockage.
Are zebra shades better than roller shades?
Zebra shades offer more versatility than traditional roller shades because you can adjust the light without fully raising or lowering them. They're ideal if you want both privacy and natural light options in one shade.
How do zebra shades work?
Zebra shades have two layers of fabric with alternating stripes. When you adjust the shade, the stripes align differently - solid to solid for privacy/blackout, or solid to sheer for filtered light. A simple pull changes the alignment.
Can zebra shades be motorized?
Yes! Our zebra shades can be motorized with battery or hardwired motors. Control them via remote, smartphone app, or voice assistants like Alexa and Google Home.
