Shipping Outside Texas

We currently focus on serving Texas customers with free shipping. If you're located outside Texas, please contact us - we may be able to accommodate your order with calculated shipping rates.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can track your package through our carrier's website to see real-time delivery updates.

Packaging

Your shades are carefully packaged to prevent damage during transit:

  • Each shade is wrapped in protective material
  • Sturdy cardboard tubes protect roller shades
  • All hardware and mounting supplies included
  • Installation instructions in every package

Delivery Issues

If your package arrives damaged or you experience any delivery issues, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. Take photos of any damage before discarding packaging materials. We'll work with you to resolve the issue promptly.
